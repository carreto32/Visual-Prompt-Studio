VOCABULARY In art, medium is the material used to create the artwork, like a paint on the wall. Meaning is the message or idea the artwork communicates to the viewer. Subject matter refers to what is actually shown in the artwork, like people, objects, or events. Representational art is art that shows real life people, places, or things in a way that is recognizable and based on reality.
