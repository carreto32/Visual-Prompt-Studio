TITTLE Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1

PROMPT CREATOR Create an image of a large-scale mural painted in acrylic on an urban building wall, designed as a form of social protest against the 2025–2026 U.S. administration. The mural is located in a busy city square and depicts Donald Trump alongside people from the United States.
The main message is to show how people are worried about the decisions made by this administration. The subject matter includes the Greenland situation, tariffs, and immigration.
The style is representational, with bold colors and a dynamic composition to emphasize contrast and movement, symbolizing the tension between the world and the U.S. administration.

VOCABULARY The artwork is a big mural painted with acrylic on a city wall. It shows people feeling worried and unsure about the decisions made by the U.S. administration.
